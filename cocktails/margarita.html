Cocktails

Margarita

      
          Ingredients

            ●  Tequila 75ml         ● Salt
            ●  Triple dry 25 ml     ● Ice
            ●  Lime juice 25 ml        

        

         Preparation
        
         1. In a shaker add the tequila, triple sec,
          lemon juice and ice. 
         2. Shake until mixed well.
