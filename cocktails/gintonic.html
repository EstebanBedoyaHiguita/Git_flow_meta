Cocktails

Gin Tonic

      
          Ingredients

            ●  50 ml gin           ●   Ice 
            ●  200 ml tonic      ●  1 slice of lime or lemon 
            ●  ball cup             ●  Optional: One or two juniper berries
        

         Preparation
        
        1. In a round glass add ice
        2. Then squeeze the juice from 1 slice of Lemon into the glass
        3. Add 50 ml of gin
        4. Add 100 ml of tonic
        5. Decorate with the lemon slice and a rosemary leaf
