Cocktails

Mojito

      
          Ingredients

            ●  40ml. white rum         ● A squeezed lime, about 30 ml. of juice
            ●  Crushed ice               ● 6 mint or peppermint leaves 
            ●  2 teaspoons of sugar    ● Soda or sparkling water
        

         Preparation
        
        1. In a glass, mix the mint leaves with the lemon 
            juice and sugar.
        2. Add the rum along with the ice cubes. Then, add 
            the mineral water and stir.
        3. Decorate with a sprig of mint and a slice of lemon.
