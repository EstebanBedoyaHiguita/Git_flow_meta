LASAGNE ALLA BOLOGNESE

It is one of the most traditional and iconic dishes of Italian gastronomy and one of the most popular worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 70 grams of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Butter
  • 300 grams of minced meat (it is juicier with a mixture of pork and veal).
  • Half a glass of white wine
  • 250 grams of canned peeled whole tomatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 50 grams of butter
  • 50 grams of flour
  • Half a liter of milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. In a saucepan heat three tablespoons of olive oil and add the finely chopped carrot and onion. When the onion is well browned add the meat and let it brown as well. Then add the glass of wine and let it evaporate. After this, add the diced seedless canned tomatoes and let the sauce cook for half an hour, adding salt and pepper to taste.
  2. While the Bolognese sauce is cooking we can make the b√©chamel sauce. In a frying pan melt the butter and add the flour. Stirring constantly, add hot milk until the sauce starts to boil. Add the salt, reduce the heat to a minimum and cook for about twenty minutes, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Season with a pinch of pepper.
  3. Boil the pasta in boiling salted water and place the sheets on a cloth. It is very important that they do not touch each other, as they will stick together.
  4. Now it is time to assemble the lasagna. In an ovenproof dish spread the base with butter and cover the base with a first layer of pasta. Add a layer of bolognese sauce, another of bechamel sauce, sprinkle grated cheese, put a little butter and another layer of pasta. Continue with the operation until you use all the ingredients, but crown the lasagna with a layer of meat, bechamel sauce and cheese (not with the pasta, as usual).
  5. Put the lasagna in the oven and cook at 180 degrees for half an hour. Buon appetit
